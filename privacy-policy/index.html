Data Protection

Privacy Policy for a Business Built on Trust.

This policy explains how Pinnacle Route collects, uses, stores, and protects information shared through our website, strategy sessions, consultation forms, and digital transformation engagements.

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Policy Sections

Overview Information We Collect How We Use Information Sharing & Service Providers Cookies & Tracking Security & Retention Your Choices Contact

1. Overview

Pinnacle Route provides AI automation, custom software, CRM and ERP development, SaaS platforms, eCommerce systems, mobile applications, UI/UX design, and related digital transformation services.

When you contact us, book a strategy session, submit a project inquiry, communicate with us on WhatsApp or email, or work with us on a client engagement, we may process personal and business information so we can respond, evaluate project fit, deliver services, and maintain a secure business relationship.

We do not sell your personal information. We use the information you share to communicate with you, understand your business needs, provide services, improve our operations, and protect our website and systems.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

We may collect information submitted through our website contact form, Calendly booking page, WhatsApp, email, calls, discovery sessions, proposals, contracts, and client onboarding processes. This may include:

  • Name, company name, job title, and business details.
  • Email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, and preferred contact method.
  • Website URL, project type, budget range, timeline, and business challenge.
  • Messages, files, requirements, feedback, meeting notes, and other information you choose to share.
  • Billing, contract, and administrative information required for client engagements.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically by hosting, analytics, security, or embedded service providers. This may include IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, pages viewed, approximate location, timestamps, and interaction data.

Project and business system data

If you engage Pinnacle Route for software, automation, CRM, ERP, SaaS, dashboard, or integration work, we may access business data, sample records, API credentials, workflow documents, system configurations, user roles, or test data only as needed to plan, build, test, support, or improve the agreed solution.

3. How We Use Information

We use information for legitimate business purposes connected to our website, consultations, and service delivery, including to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule strategy sessions.
  • Assess your business needs and recommend suitable services or solutions.
  • Prepare proposals, estimates, timelines, project plans, and contracts.
  • Design, develop, test, deliver, maintain, and improve digital systems.
  • Communicate about project updates, support requests, billing, and account matters.
  • Improve our website, content, forms, customer experience, and internal workflows.
  • Protect against spam, fraud, misuse, unauthorized access, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, contractual, and regulatory obligations.

4. Sharing & Service Providers

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website, communicate with prospects and clients, process inquiries, host systems, manage bookings, provide security, deliver projects, or run business operations.

These providers may include website hosting platforms, backend form processors, email services, calendar scheduling tools, WhatsApp or communication platforms, Google reCAPTCHA, analytics tools, cloud infrastructure providers, payment or accounting tools, and project management systems.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to enforce agreements, to protect our rights or security, or in connection with a business restructuring, merger, or transfer where appropriate safeguards apply.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website and third-party tools may use cookies, scripts, pixels, logs, or similar technologies to support basic site functionality, secure forms, prevent spam, understand website usage, and improve the visitor experience.

For example, Google reCAPTCHA may analyze visitor interactions to help distinguish real users from automated abuse. Scheduling, social, or communication links may also set cookies or collect information when you interact with those services.

You can usually adjust cookie settings through your browser. Blocking certain technologies may affect website functionality, form submissions, spam protection, or embedded services.

6. Security & Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These safeguards may include restricted access, secure communication channels, role-based permissions, backups, monitoring, and careful handling of client system credentials.

No website, internet transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information through general website forms unless we specifically request it through an appropriate secure channel.

We retain information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including inquiry follow-up, service delivery, support, legal compliance, accounting, dispute resolution, and business recordkeeping. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the nature of our relationship with you.

7. Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction on the use of your personal information. You may also ask us to stop contacting you for non-essential communications.

To make a privacy request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity and understand the context of your request before taking action. Some information may need to be retained where required for legal, contractual, security, or legitimate business reasons.

If your information was shared with us by your employer, organization, or another client contact as part of a business engagement, we may coordinate with that organization where appropriate.
